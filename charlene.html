Peaceful and Fresh Air

People plant this to protect the sea

Make our sea beautyfull and protected by us. Mangroves are special trees that live in salty water near the coast. They have roots that help stop the land from washing away, provide a safe place for baby fish to grow, clean the water around them, and even help fight against storms. Mangroves are like nature's superheroes for the coastal environment.
